What is ZOMI?

ZOMI is a 2D side-scrolling action-survival game built entirely in C with SDL2, inspired by the novel and film World War Z. A zombie pandemic has shattered civilization. Streets are overrun, walls have fallen, and the last survivors must fight, puzzle, and escape across four desperate acts.

Play solo or team up in local co-op with a friend. Use your fists, your feet, or your gun. Solve puzzles, hack terminals, escape on a crane, survive at 30,000 feet — and finally reach the laboratory where it all began.

The game supports a custom ESP32 wireless controller alongside keyboard & mouse, delivering a unique physical gameplay experience built from scratch.

Mechanics

How to Play

✊

Combat System

Three-slot inventory: punch, kick, and firearm. Cycle weapons with 1 2 3 or the controller's L/R buttons. Execute with F or the red button.

❤

Health & Healing

Each player has 3 lives. Pick up bandages to heal. The HUD shows hearts and ammo at all times. Lose all lives — face the Second Chance quiz to come back.

👥

Solo & Co-op

Play alone or with a friend on the same machine. In co-op, each player gets their own viewport, health bar, and input method.

💻

Puzzles & Mini-games

Hack terminals with a timing mini-game. Solve logic enigmas and quizzes. Answer correctly to earn a Second Chance after death. Every level hides a unique puzzle mechanic.

ESP32 Controller

A custom wireless gamepad built on ESP32 communicates via UDP over WiFi. Joystick, D-pad, four action buttons, L/R triggers — fully mapped for every level. No external library required.

🎯

Scoring

A score system tracks kills, time, and puzzles solved. Top 3 scores are saved locally and displayed on the leaderboard at the end of each run.

Characters

Player 1

Gerry

Former UN investigator. Fast, resourceful, and terrifyingly calm under pressure. Specializes in close-quarters combat.

Player 2

Karin

Combat medic. Carries extra bandages and knows how to use them. Slower but tougher — the backbone of any duo run.

Enemy

The Infected

Once human. Now relentless. They sprint, they swarm, and they don't stop. Take them down before they take you down.

Boss

Patient Zero

The original host. Larger, faster, and far more dangerous than anything you've faced. The antidote is worthless without his keycard.
