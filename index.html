The World

What is ZOMI?

ZOMI is a 2D side-scrolling action-survival game built entirely in C with SDL2, inspired by the novel and film World War Z. A zombie pandemic has shattered civilization. Streets are overrun, walls have fallen, and the last survivors must fight, puzzle, and escape across four desperate acts.

Play solo or team up in local co-op with a friend. Crack skulls with your fists, your feet, or your gun. Hack terminals, swing from cranes, fall out of airplanes, infiltrate the laboratory where the outbreak began — and reach the only antidote left on Earth.

ZOMI ships with three input methods: keyboard & mouse, a PS4 / Xbox controller (via SDL_GameController), and a fully custom ESP32 wireless gamepad built from scratch — communicating over UDP/WiFi with no third-party library. All three are first-class, with on-screen tutorials adapting to whichever you plug in.

Mechanics

How to Play

✊

Combat System

Three-slot inventory: punch, kick, and firearm. Cycle weapons with 1 2 3, the ESP32 trigger, or the PS4 L1 / R1 shoulders. Execute with F, the red button, or PS4 Square.

❤

Health & Healing

Each player has 5 lives. Pick up bandages to heal. When your health drops to 3, an in-world tutorial reminds you which button heals on your current input. Lose all lives â€” face the Second Chance quiz to come back.

👥

Solo & Split-Screen Co-op

Play alone or with a friend on the same machine. In co-op, each player gets their own viewport, health bar, and input method. A small badge in each half-screen shows at a glance which controller you're holding.

🧠

Adaptive Tutorials

On-screen prompts auto-detect your input. Press the right shape for your controller — PS4 button icons on a PS4 pad, custom colored circles on the ESP32 pad, or keyboard hints on a keyboard. Sprint hint appears after your first jump and fades when you sprint.

💻

Puzzles & Mini-games

Hack city terminals with a flux-routing mini-game. Solve a drag-and-drop image puzzle to earn a Second Chance after dying in the airplane. Answer Second Chance quizzes after death in the city. Every level hides a unique mechanic.

🎬

Cinematics & Death FX

MP4 cinematics between acts via libavcodec, with PTS-synced audio and a 1.8× soft-knee audio boost so dialogue cuts through. On death, the camera zooms in on your character, the screen tints red, and "WASTED" punches through — before you face your second-chance challenge.

ESP32 Controller

A custom wireless gamepad built on ESP32 communicates via UDP over WiFi. Joystick, four action buttons, L1/R1 shoulders, click-to-sprint — fully mapped for every level.

PS4 / Xbox Controller

Plug any PS4 or Xbox-compatible pad. SDL_GameController handles the mapping. Tutorials show the exact □ △ ✕ ◯ buttons of your pad.

🎯

Scoring & Save

A score system tracks kills, time, and puzzles solved. Top-3 scores are saved locally and displayed on the leaderboard at the end of each run. Auto-save after each completed act so you don't lose progress.

Cast

Characters

Player 1

Gerry

Former UN investigator. Fast, resourceful, and terrifyingly calm under pressure. Specializes in close-quarters combat.

Player 2

Karin

Combat medic. Carries extra bandages and knows how to use them. Slower but tougher — the backbone of any duo run.

Enemy

The Infected

Once human. Now relentless. They sprint, they swarm, and they don't stop. Two variants — male sprinter and female brawler. Take them down before they take you down.

Boss

Patient Zero

The original host. Larger, faster, and far more dangerous than anything you've faced. The antidote is worthless without his keycard.
